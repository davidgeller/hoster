About

HOSTER

Lightweight Web Hosting Platform

Hoster is a self-hosted web hosting platform that turns any device into a web server. Upload a ZIP file and your site is live — with versioning, SPA support, analytics, and free HTTPS via Cloudflare Tunnel.

No cloud providers, no monthly fees, no vendor lock-in. Just your code, your device, your domain.
